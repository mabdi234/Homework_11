What happens as you get closer to the Equator

Weather Data Visualization

For this project we were asked to analyze how the weather changed as we got closer to the equator. We took weather data from 500 random cities and used Matplotlib to plot the data. We decided to look at how weather changed as you got closer to the equator in four categories. Max Temperature, Wind Speed, Cloudiness, and Humidity. On the site there are four visualizations, a comparison page and a table of the data we got.
